BunnyPop is a charming school project implemented by @seinlei from Edinburgh Napier University. BunnyPop is all about spreading joy and fun. Dive into a world filled with adorable bunnies and cute surprises, all designed with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Don't forget to check out our random jokes for an extra dose of laughter.

We've got some awesome plans in the works! Soon, you'll be able to compete on leaderboards, personalize your experience with user accounts, and enjoy animated characters. Get excited for even more fun and excitement coming your way! Keep an eye out for updates!
